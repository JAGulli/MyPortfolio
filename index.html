Hiya! I'm Jodi Gullickson

My Skills

User Research

User research is the basis for the rest of what I do as a designer. I take pride in learning as much as I can about how users use the products, as well as their motivations and general attitudes relevant to the project. Digital products that bring people back are delightful, thoughtful & useful. User research helps designers understand what we need to deliver just that.

Qualitiative Interviews
